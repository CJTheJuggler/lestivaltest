About Us

Welcome to Lestival, Leicester's premier annual circus festival! We invite you to immerse yourself in a world of wonder, creativity, and excitement as we celebrate the vibrant spirit of the circus in our beautiful city.

At lestival, you can explore a variety of engaging workshops led by experienced circus professionals. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned performer, our sessions offer something for everyone. Get hands-on experience and discover your inner performer in a fun, supportive environment.

Don’t miss our festival shop, where you can find an array of circus equipment to enhance your own circus journey. Whether you’re looking to equip yourself for the next performance or searching for a special gift, we have everything you need to bring the circus home!

As the day winds down, prepare for our thrilling evening show, featuring spectacular performances from talented artists that will leave you on the edge of your seat. Experience the magic of the circus as acts captivate audiences of all ages.

Join us at lestival for a day filled with laughter, learning, and unforgettable memories. We can’t wait to share the joy of the circus with you!
